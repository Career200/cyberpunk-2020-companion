The world of Cyberpunk is a combination of savage, sophisticateed, modern and retrograde. Even more so in 30 years since the system and the world were created.

Think advanced tech and limitless possibilities... but one gigabyte of storage is "ought to be enough for anybody.". Hard-boiled scary-looking metalheads... That piss their pants when they see their idol on a chrome-rock concert. Barely any uncurable illnesses left, and anti-ageing technology offers near immortality... But daily death rate is as high as it was 200 years ago - mostly thanks to violent crime
